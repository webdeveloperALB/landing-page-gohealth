GoHealth Albania - Premium Dental Clinic in Albania

Welcome to GoHealth Albania, your trusted dental clinic offering premium quality dental care at competitive prices. Located in the heart of Albania, we combine expert professionals, state-of-the-art technology, and personalized care to deliver exceptional dental services to both local and international patients. Our clinic has been serving patients from across Europe for over a decade, establishing itself as a leader in the dental tourism industry in Albania.

Why Choose GoHealth Albania?

With years of experience in dental tourism, we understand the needs of patients seeking affordable yet high-quality dental treatments. Our multilingual staff ensures seamless communication, while our modern facilities meet international standards of care and hygiene. Every member of our team is committed to providing personalized care tailored to your specific dental needs.

Our clinical team consists of specialists with international training and certifications, ensuring you receive care that meets or exceeds European standards. We pride ourselves on staying current with the latest advancements in dental technology and techniques through continuous professional development and investment in cutting-edge equipment.

Cost-Effective Premium Care

Experience significant savings compared to Western European prices without compromising on quality. Our treatments cost up to 70% less than equivalent procedures in Italy, Germany, or the UK, making quality dental care accessible to everyone. These savings are possible due to the lower operational costs in Albania while maintaining European standards of dental materials and technology.

For example, a single dental implant that might cost €2,000-3,000 in Western Europe is available at GoHealth Albania for approximately €700-900, including the abutment and crown. Similarly, zirconia crowns that cost €800-1,200 in Italy or Germany are available at our clinic for €300-400.

Our Comprehensive Dental Services

Advanced Implantology

Dental Implants: Our specialist team excels in implantology, offering single tooth replacements to full-mouth rehabilitations including the advanced All-on-4 and All-on-6 techniques. We use only premium implant systems with lifetime warranties, giving you peace of mind about your investment. Our success rate for dental implants exceeds 98%, thanks to our careful planning process that includes 3D scans and customized surgical guides.

For patients requiring bone augmentation prior to implant placement, we offer various bone grafting procedures and sinus lift surgery, all performed by our experienced oral surgeons in a sterile operating environment.

Aesthetic Dentistry

Dental Crowns: We provide various crown options including metal-ceramic, zirconia, and e.max crowns, all manufactured in our partner laboratory to ensure perfect fit and aesthetics. Our digital workflow allows for precise impressions and manufacturing, reducing the need for adjustments and ensuring long-lasting results. Each crown is custom-designed to match your natural teeth in color, shape, and function.

Dental Veneers: Transform your smile with our custom porcelain veneers, designed to correct minor misalignments, discoloration, or chips. Our minimally invasive approach preserves as much natural tooth structure as possible while delivering stunning cosmetic results. We offer both traditional porcelain veneers and no-prep options for suitable candidates.

General and Preventive Dentistry

Teeth Whitening: Achieve a brighter smile with our professional whitening treatments, customized to your specific needs and shade preferences. We offer both in-office power whitening for immediate results and take-home kits for gradual whitening at your convenience. Our whitening procedures are designed to minimize sensitivity while maximizing effectiveness.

Root Canal Treatment: Our endodontic specialists use microscope-assisted techniques to perform precise and comfortable root canal treatments, saving teeth that might otherwise require extraction. We utilize rotary endodontic instruments and biocompatible filling materials to ensure the best possible outcomes.

Periodontal Treatment: Maintain healthy gums with our comprehensive periodontal care, including deep cleaning, laser therapy for gum disease, and surgical interventions when necessary. Our hygienists provide detailed oral hygiene instructions tailored to your specific needs.

Consultations and Planning

Free Check-up Consultations: Start with our comprehensive examination and personalized treatment plan, with no obligation and no hidden costs. During this appointment, we conduct a thorough oral examination, take necessary X-rays, and discuss all treatment options with transparent pricing.

Digital Smile Design: Visualize your new smile before treatment begins with our digital smile design technology. This allows you to provide input and make adjustments to achieve your desired aesthetic result.
